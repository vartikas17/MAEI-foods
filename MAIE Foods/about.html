Who We Are

A Taste of Authentic MAIE Pasta

MAIE FOODS is committed to bringing a wide range of delicious pasta options that suit every taste and recipe. With multiple shapes and perfect texture, our pasta is designed to enhance every dish — whether it's a simple home-cooked meal or a professional kitchen creation.

Our Journey

From Grain to Plate

The Beginning

A Passion for Real Pasta

MAIE Foods was founded with a simple belief: every family deserves pasta made with honest ingredients. We started in Kiratpur Industrial Area, Itarsi, with a commitment to No Maida and 100% Durum Wheat Semolina.

Growth

Expanding Our Range

From classic Penne and Macaroni to nutritious blends with Quinoa, Beetroot, and Multi Millet — we expanded our portfolio to serve health-conscious families and professional kitchens alike.

Today

Trusted Across India

Today, MAIE Foods serves thousands of happy customers, restaurants, and retailers. Our modern production facility ensures every batch meets the highest standards of freshness, quality, and taste.
